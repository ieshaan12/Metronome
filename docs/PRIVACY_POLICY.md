# Privacy Policy — OK Metronomer

**Last updated:** March 24, 2026

## Overview

OK Metronomer ("the App") is a free, open-source metronome application for Android developed by ieshaan12.

## Data Collection

The App does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- **No usage analytics** or crash reporting is collected
- **No network requests** are made — the App works entirely offline
- **No cookies, identifiers, or tracking technologies** are used
- **No third-party SDKs** that collect data are included
- **No data is shared** with any third party

## Permissions

The App requests only one permission:

- **Vibrate** — Used solely to provide haptic feedback on each metronome beat. No data is collected through this permission.

## Data Storage

The App does not store any user data on your device or on any remote server. All settings (BPM, time signature) exist only in memory during the current session and are not persisted.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
